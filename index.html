About Shree Shuba Associates

Company Profile

Shree Shuba Associates was established in 2010 under the proprietorship of Mr. A.C.S. Mutho, who brings over 26 years of rich professional experience in the shipping trade and international global logistics. With a vision to deliver global expertise locally, our founder has been instrumental in the growth of several multinational logistics companies in India, including SWG Group (Air Link India Ltd), SDV Logistics, Cargo Partner Logistics Pvt Ltd, and Unique Logistics India Pvt Ltd.

Headquartered in Chennai, we have expanded our network to five locations: Tuticorin, Sricity, Krishnapatnam Port, Mumbai, and Chennai, with plans to open in Pune. Our young and dynamic team is dedicated to handling challenging jobs and providing exceptional service to our clients and partners.

Business Plan & Services

We act as a liaison between corporate companies and customs departments for both air and sea operations, serving manufacturing, trading, and various industry verticals. Our unique value lies in our advisory and mentorship for exporters and importers, helping clients maximize government export benefit schemes and navigate complex global trade processes.

  • Ocean & Air Exports/Imports
  • Customs Clearance & Consultancy
  • Mentorship for Startup Exporters/Importers
  • Government Export Benefits Schemes Processing
  • Shipping Advisory & Consulting under one umbrella

Vision & Mission

To establish a global Indian MNC with our own network of global operations, delivering world-class logistics solutions and building long-lasting relationships with our clients.

Industry Expertise

We serve a wide range of industries including Automotive, Electronics, Garments, Leather, Trading, Industrial Projects, Agriculture, Food Products, Perishables (fruits, vegetables, flowers, marine products), and more.

Key Differentiators
